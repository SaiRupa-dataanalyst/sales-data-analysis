# 📊 Sales Performance and Revenue Analysis

## 📌 Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance and revenue trends across different regions, categories, and time periods to generate meaningful business insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Excel  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance using key metrics and visualizations:

### 🔹 Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 584.49K  
- Average Sales: 1.95K  
- Total Orders: 300  
- Active Customers: 50  
- Total Customers: 50  

---

## 📊 Visualizations Included

### 1. Sales by Region
- Compares total sales across West, North, South, and East regions  
- West region shows slightly higher sales performance  

### 2. Customer Distribution by Region
- West has the highest number of customers  
- North has the lowest customer count  

### 3. Monthly Sales Trend
- Peak sales observed in **July (0.12M)**  
- Lowest sales in **August (0.01M)**  
- Fluctuating trend indicates seasonal impact  

### 4. Sales by Product
- Some products contribute significantly higher revenue (~62K)  
- Helps identify high-performing products  

### 5. Sales by Category
- Electronics contribute the highest share (~43.88%)  
- Clothing has the lowest contribution (~10.68%)  

---

## 🎯 Key Insights
- West region leads in both sales and customer count  
- Sales show strong growth mid-year, especially in July  
- Electronics category dominates overall revenue  
- Certain products drive a large portion of total sales  
- Seasonal variations impact monthly performance  

---

## 📌 Features
- Interactive filters (Category, Region, Order Date)  
- Dynamic visuals for better decision-making  
- Clean and user-friendly dashboard layout  

---

## 🚀 Outcome
This project helps businesses:
- Identify high-performing regions and products  
- Understand customer distribution  
- Track sales trends over time  
- Make data-driven decisions
